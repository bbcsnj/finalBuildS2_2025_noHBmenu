Ben Roethlisberger

Born: March 2, 1982
Height: 6′ 5″
Weight: 245

Biography:

Ben Roethlisberger played 18 seasons NFL with the Pittsburgh Steelers. Before the NFL he played college footbal at Miami University in Ohio from 2000 to 2003. in the He earned the NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year Award in 2004. In 2006, he became the youngest Super Bowl–winning quarterback in NFL history, winning Super Bowl XL 21–10 over the Seattle Seahawks at the age of 23. In 2008, he led the Steelers to another championship over over the Arizona Cardinals with an 88 yard drive. He led his team to another Super Bowl appearance in 2010, but came up short against the Green Bay Packers. After another decade of play he finally announced his retirement in 2022. He holds many franchise and NFL records including the NFL record for Most touchdown passes in consecutive games with 12.

Notable Achievements

  • NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year in 2004.

  • Two Super Bowl wins
  • NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year award in 2004
